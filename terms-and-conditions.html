5. Summary of Mechanics:

a. Join Lazada’s “Laz Bingo Live” and win Real Deals from June 3-6, 2025! b. Everyday from June 3-6, 2025, 6:00 PM, Lazada will host a daily Laz Bingo Live  Pinoy bingo game (“Live”) via its Lazada Live platform. During the Live, eligible

participants can play their Laz Bingo Card and watch as Bingo numbers are  drawn one by one in the Live. The first six (6) participants to complete the Bingo  pattern of the day and submit their winning Laz Bingo Card will get one of six (6)  Real Deal prizes of the day.

c. To be able to join the Laz Bingo Live of that day, Participants must first post on  their social media account the Bingo Card of the day they received from  www.LazBingoLive.com

d. The six (6) winners of the Real Deal prize of the day will be validated on the spot  and announced during the Live.

6. How To Join:

a. Participant must generate their unique Laz Bingo Card of the day through the  Bingo Card Generator microsite (http://www.LazBingoLive.com) to be able to  play in the Laz Bingo Live game of that same day.

b. Users must input the following details to be able to generate their unique daily  Laz Bingo Card for that day:  

i. First Name

ii. Last Name

iii. Lazada Email

c. Participants can only generate one (1) unique LazBingo Card per day. However,  they can generate a new LazBingo Card the next day and play in the next day’s  LazBingo Live.

d. Participants may only generate the Bingo Cards during the following times: i. June 3, 2025 - 12:00 midnight - 5:59pm

ii. June 4, 2025 - 12:00 midnight - 5:59pm

iii. June 5, 2025 - 12:00 midnight - 5:59pm

iv. June 6, 2025 - 12:00 midnight - 5:59pm

e. Once the Bingo Card has been generated, participants must post the Bingo Card  on their chosen social media platform – Facebook, X, Instagram, Tiktok – using  the hashtag #LazBingoLive and tag the official Lazada account on their caption  for their Bingo Card to be considered valid.

i. The post must be set to public.

ii. The post must be made before 6:00pm of that day, as the LazBingo  Live will start 6:00pm of that same day. Posts made after 6:00pm and/or  the start of the LazBingo Live will be deemed invalid and will not be  

eligible to win that day.

f. The LazBingo Card generated on that day can only be played in the same day’s  LazBingo Live.

7. How to Play:

a. Once a Participant has successfully posted their Laz Bingo Card on their social  page and followed all criteria, they are eligible to play in the Laz Bingo Live at  6:00 PM of that same day.

b. To play, Participant must tune in to the Laz Bingo Live found in the Lazada app,  from June 3-6, 2025, 6:00 PM, to play the Bingo game. They must wait for a

winning Bingo pattern to be completed based on the Bingo numbers drawn during the Live.

c. Once a winning Bingo pattern is completed, participants must fill up the Microsite  form (lzd.co/LazBingoLiveBingoWinners), wherein they will have to submit the ff  details for our verification:

i. Full Name

ii. Email Address

iii. Home Address

iv. Link to their Bingo Card social media post

d. Selection and validation of winners from the Form submissions will be done  during the LazBingo Live. The first 6 Participants who submitted valid winning  entries will be declared the Winners:

i. Full Name and Email submitted in the Laz Bingo Live’s Form matches the  Full Name and Email they submitted when generating that Bingo card. ii. The unique Bingo Card code found in their social media post matches the  code generated in www.LazBingoLive.com

iii. Must post their Bingo Card on their chosen social media platform before  6:00 PM of that day.

iv. Must use the hashtag #LazBingoLive in the caption

v. Must tag the Lazada official account, @lazadaph.

vi. Must tune in to the LazBingo Live to play the game and generate a  winning Bingo pattern based on the numbers already drawn at the time of  submission.

e. Participants must only submit in the Form if they have completed a winning Bingo  pattern based on the bingo numbers drawn at the time of submission. If a Bingo  Card is submitted without a winning Bingo pattern, the Bingo Card becomes  disqualified for that day, regardless if a winning Bingo pattern is formed later on.  Participant can play again the next day.

f. The first six (6) Participants with valid winning entries, as submitted in the Form  during the Laz Bingo Live, will be announced during the Live as the six (6) Real  Deal winners of the day.
